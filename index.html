About The Attic

Our Mission

Going into the business, The Attic was just an unattainable idea. But through hard work and dedication, we were able to make our dream a reality. The Attic was created with the purpose of helping lower the rate of waste and grow peoples love for reading.

The Attic Founders
Mia, Emily, & Zoe

What We Do

Many questions are asked about what exaclty we sell. At the Attic we take used, forgotten books and repurpose them. We wrap them in ecofriendly paper and then place them into a category. With a short description of the book, you can choose which one to get! After all the excitment you get to finally unwrap your book.
